## Short description (the one-liner under the title)
A survival wildfire-watch game in one HTML file. Assemble solar towers from parts you
haul on your back, fly drones that hunt the first curl of smoke, feed yourself, tend
your machines, and turn rescued strangers into a community of watchers — across ten
regions of one Range. Every mechanic is real: the tower you build is a real Raspberry
Pi fire-detection node, fictionalized.

## The three bullets
- **The network is the game.** Panels foul, batteries brown out at night, rotors wear,
  storms take antennas — a fire you miss burns forest that stays burned. The save file
  *is* the forest.
- **Survival that serves the watch.** Hunger, thirst, warmth, stamina — and the same
  drones that hunt smoke spot the elk you stalk downwind for food.
- **Ten regions, one year.** Living wind, night fires, storms, snow, drought embers,
  dry lightning, a closed parts economy, and a fire-season finale that tests everything
  and everyone you've built.
